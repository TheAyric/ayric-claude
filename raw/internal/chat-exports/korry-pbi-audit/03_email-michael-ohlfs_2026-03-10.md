## Lessons Learned

- **Pre-kickoff context matters for client communications.** Mentioning a conference or limited availability before an engagement starts is responsible expectation-setting, not a red flag — distinct from mid-engagement availability issues.
- Ayric's stated preference was to keep the conference mention in the response
- Final revision focused on tightening language and professionalism rather than restructuring the message
- Ayric prefers prose over bullet points and dashes in professional emails
- Formal register matched to the recipient's level (executive vs. technical colleague)
