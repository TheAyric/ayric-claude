## Key Tax Compliance Issue

Korry's PO included a tax-exempt resale certificate (Resale # A03 347625), but Claude flagged that resale certificates are designed for goods or services being resold to end customers — not for internally consumed consulting services. Since WA retail sales tax applies to IT consulting services under ESSB 5814 (effective October 2025), Ayric decided to apply sales tax to the invoice.

**Key learning:** Resale certificates don't cover internally consumed services. Korry's tax-exempt resale certificate was not applicable to this consulting engagement.
