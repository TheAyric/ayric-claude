## Summary

Ayric is a data and analytics consultant building a new consultancy, currently pursuing CPMAI certification. His expertise spans Power BI, data architecture, data governance, and data quality, and he is expanding into AI readiness, integration consulting, prompt engineering, and early-stage agent development. He already owns ayric.com and was seeking help creating a brandable company name that incorporates his first name, signals data and analytics work, and appeals to CEOs and CIOs looking to advance their AI capabilities — explicitly not "tech bro" in tone.

Through a structured naming process, Ayric gravitated toward "Ayric Analytics" initially, then expanded the brief to include AI signaling as his service mix grows. After exploring options like Ayric Intelligence, Ayric Foresight, and Ayric Analytics & AI, he landed on **Ayric Intelligence** as his company name.

## Key Decisions

- **Company name:** Ayric Intelligence
- **Key insight:** The initials spell **A.I.**, creating a built-in double meaning with strong marketing potential
  - Taglines: "Get A.I. working for you" / "Need A.I.? You found it."
- **Domain architecture:**
  - **ayric.ai** → Ayric Intelligence (the consultancy)
  - **ayric.com** → Personal brand
- **ayric.ai domain:** Available via Cloudflare at ~$70/year with required two-year minimum upfront registration
