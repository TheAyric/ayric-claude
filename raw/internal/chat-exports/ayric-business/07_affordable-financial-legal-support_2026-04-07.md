## Financial Support Recommendations

- **Bookkeeping:** Solo consultancy at this revenue level doesn't need a full CPA firm. Look for a bookkeeper specializing in sole proprietors or small LLCs — many work virtually at $100–300/month.
- **Tax prep:** An Enrolled Agent (EA) is often half the cost of a CPA with the same IRS representation rights. Prioritize someone familiar with Washington B&O and retail sales tax for services.
- **Tools:** Wave (free) or QuickBooks Self-Employed (cheap) paired with a quarterly EA review.
- **Free resources:** SCORE mentors (Seattle/Snohomish chapter is active), Washington SBDC (free consulting hours).

## Legal Support Recommendations

- **Trademark filing (Ayric Intelligence, Classes 35 & 42):**
  - DIY via USPTO TEAS Plus (~$250/class)
  - Flat-fee trademark attorney ($500–800 total)
- **General contract/structure questions:** Prepaid legal services (LegalShield) or solo business attorney with fixed-fee consultations. Many offer free 30-minute intros.
