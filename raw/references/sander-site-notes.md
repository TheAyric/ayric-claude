# Slides by Sander — site notes

Source: https://www.slidesbysander.com/
Fetched: 2026-04-17 via Claude in Chrome

## What he sells
- **Templates** — €199+, drag-and-drop PowerPoint files with "hundreds of slide layouts"
- **Courses** — commercial, hosted on Teachable (slides-by-sander.teachable.com)
- **Custom work / coaching** — on-demand design service

## Credentials shown
- 10+ years experience
- 456+ YouTube tutorials
- 10,000+ slides made
- 100% positive reviews (self-reported)

## What's accessible without purchase
The homepage is marketing. His actual design frameworks (master-slide architecture, morph storytelling patterns, typography systems) are behind the template/course paywalls.

## Signal for us
- Sander is a legit professional PowerPoint designer, not an influencer-grifter
- His POTX template system likely teaches the master-slide discipline we'd want to encode into the Compex template
- YouTube videos are the free-tier reference — worth sampling a few for master-slide and morph patterns before deciding whether to buy a template/course

## Takeaway
Do not plan to scrape Sander for patterns. If you want his system, buy a template. Otherwise reference only — cite, don't replicate.
