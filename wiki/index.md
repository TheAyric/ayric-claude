## How to navigate

- Every page should link to its siblings and any related concepts elsewhere.
- Use Obsidian's graph view (`Ctrl+G`) to see the shape of the knowledge.
- Search (`Ctrl+O`) by filename; use full-text search (`Ctrl+Shift+F`) when the term isn't in a title.
